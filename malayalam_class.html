മലയാളം പാഠശാല

Classes will be held on alternate Saturdays.

Parents can enroll more than one child for the lessons. Each session will last an hour and half, instructors will use simple but effective techniques such as story-telling based on Kerala’s folklore and classics. This would keep kids enthused & engaged through out the session. The goal of the program is to help children make progress in reading, writing, and speaking the Malayalam language. The kids will also be given chance to express their skills in various programs as drama, skit, mono act and speech presentation.

Classes on every 1st and 3rd Saturdays at Bear, and every 2nd and 4th Saturday at Hockessin Temple.

We are having classes at both locations to make it convenient for our students and parents. Since both the classes are running on the same curriculum the two centers will give the children an opportunity to make up for a missed class at the other location.
