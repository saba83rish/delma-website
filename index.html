Delaware Malayalee Association (Delma)

is a cultural non profit organization of people hailing from great state of Kerala also known as God's Own Country.Delma is based out of Delaware and bridges families of Kerala origin spread all over Delaware and surrounding areas of Maryland, New Jersey and Pennsylvania. We organize numerous cultural activities all around the year. And make it possible for the continued expression of the Malayalee heritage to one and all, especially to the younger generation. Kerala claims to have a culture the history of which runs into the dim recesses of antiquity. Delma promotes and propagates Kerala culture by conducting pristine cultural programs and organizing get-together and picnics. . The mission of Delma is to serve as a forum to facilitate and enable social and cultural interactions among the member families and to motivate the kids to learn Malayalam language and Kerala traditions.
