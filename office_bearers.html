Delma Office Bearers

Delaware Malayalee Association (DELMA) is a registered non-profit, non-religious organization that aims to unite malayalees in Delware and educate the next generation of the rich cultural heritage of Kerala. Delma also seeks to foster good relations within our diverse American community through mutual friendship and cultural exchange. Delma is not owned by an individual or run by a handful of people. Every year before the second week of May Delma conducts a general body meeting. In this meeting all the Members of Delma are invited. And these members select the office bearers (also known as committee) to run the organization for the new financial year. Delma has a set of by laws or guidelines and strictly follows it to elect these committee members and to be followed in functioning of Delma.
